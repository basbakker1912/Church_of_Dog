Backpacks extend your carrying capacity, in exchange for reduced accessibilty.

# Basic types
#BASIC 
## Small backpack
- Volume: 20
- Weight: 5kg

## Medium backpack
- Volume: 35
- Weight: 10kg
- Reduced dexterity (-2)
## Large backpack
- Volume: 50
- Weight: 20kg
- Reduced dexterity (-4)
## Medical backpack
- Volume: 10
- Weight: 5kg
- Does not use a turn to grab items from.

Backpacks extend your carrying capacity, in exchange for reduced accessibility.

# Basic types
#BASIC 
## Small backpack
- Extends Volume: 20
- Volume: 5
- Weight: 2kg
## Medium backpack
- Extends Volume: 35
- Volume: 10
- Weight: 4kg
- Reduced athletics (-1)
## Large backpack
- Extends Volume: 50
- Volume: 15
- Weight: 6kg
- Reduced athletics (-2)
## Medical backpack
- Extends Volume: 10
- Volume: 5
- Weight: 2kg
- Does not use a turn to grab items from.
- Cuts medical item weight and volume in half
